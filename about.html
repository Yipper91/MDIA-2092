About Us

Welcome to Timeless Treasures, the ultimate destination for vintage enthusiasts and thrift lovers alike. Nestled in the heart of Vancouver, we are a treasure trove of unique, handpicked vintage finds that promise to add a touch of timeless charm to your wardrobe and home. Our journey began in 2024, born out of a passion for sustainable fashion and a deep appreciation for the stories that vintage items carry with them. What started as a small stall at local flea markets has blossomed into a thriving online platform, connecting vintage gems with their new guardians across the globe. At timelesstreasures.com, we believe that every piece has a story to tell. From retro fashion to classic home decor, our collection is carefully curated to ensure that each item not only brings style but also history and character into your life. We are committed to promoting sustainable shopping practices by giving these beautiful pieces a second chance to shine.
